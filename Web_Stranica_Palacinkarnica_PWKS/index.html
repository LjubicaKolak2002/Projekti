ABOUT US

Pancake house is the best pancake house in town.It is located in Vukovarska Street in Split
At the beginning, we only offered simple and traditional recipes, because we didn't
have the courage to offer you something new and experimental.
But from 2020 things are changing. Today we attract guests with a
large selection of both sweet and savory pancakes.
Another interesting thing is that you can make your own perfect pancake.
This is especially useful for people with a "special" palate or "picky" palate.
In any case, regardless of whether you belong to the group
of sweet or savory lovers, you should visit us!
